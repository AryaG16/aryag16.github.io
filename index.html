I am a PhD scholar in Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore, advised by Dr. Sidharth Sharma (IIT Jodhpur) and Dr. Ayan Mondal (IIT Indore). My work sits at the intersection of AI/ML systems and data-center networking — building network-aware frameworks for large-scale distributed DNN training. I use real-time telemetry such as latency, loss, and link utilization to adapt routing, multicast, and collective scheduling, toward scalable and sustainable networks for AI.
